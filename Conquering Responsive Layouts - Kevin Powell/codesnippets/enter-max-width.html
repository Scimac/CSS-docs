Does this make your head turn...??

According to the design principles, the width on the content should not be too long for the viewer, so much as to make his head turn. We may loose interest of the person if he turns his head so much that, it goes out of the screen and to some other interesting thing. In order to tackle this unwanted situation, add max width property to always have our content in the viewers eyespan.

Add max-width:1000px;
